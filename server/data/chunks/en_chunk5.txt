## 5. Device Submission Requirements (Receiving Phones)
- **Proof of Purchase:** An original receipt may be required.
- **Lock Status:** We do **NOT** accept devices locked to a specific carrier or those with activation locks (e.g., iCloud/Google lock).
- **Accounts & Passwords:** - All existing accounts must be logged out. 
    - **All passwords and screen locks MUST be removed** before submission to allow our technicians access for testing.
