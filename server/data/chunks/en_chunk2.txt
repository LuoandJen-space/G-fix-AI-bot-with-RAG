## 2. Repair, Replace, and Refund Policy
- **Service Hierarchy:** 
- Our priority is to **Repair** first. If a repair is not possible, we will **Replace** the device. If a replacement is unavailable, a **Full Refund** will be issued.
- **Change of Mind:** G-Fix Solution reserves the right to **refuse a refund** if the customer simply changes their mind.
- **Restocking Fee:** All eligible non-defective returns are subject to a **15% restocking fee**.
