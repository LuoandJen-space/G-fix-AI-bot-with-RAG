## 1. Warranty Exclusions
- **Physical and Water Damage:** 
- Physical damage (e.g., broken screens, bent devices) and water damage are **NOT** covered by the warranty.
    - If a device is received with pre-existing physical damage, the warranty is void.
- **Third-Party Repairs:** - Any unauthorized repairs or tampering by third parties without prior agreement from G-Fix Solution will **invalidate** the warranty.
