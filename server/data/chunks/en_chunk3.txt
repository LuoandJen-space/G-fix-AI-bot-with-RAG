## 3. NZ Consumer Guarantees Act (CGA)
- We comply with the **Consumer Guarantees Act 1993**. 
- Customers are entitled to a remedy (repair, replacement, or refund) if goods fail to meet guarantees.
- For "substantial" failures, you may reject the goods and choose a replacement or refund.
- Rights under the CGA are not limited by a fixed time but depend on the nature and price of the product.
