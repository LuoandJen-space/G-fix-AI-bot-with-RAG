# G-Fix Solution Warranty & Terms of Service

## 1. Warranty Exclusions
- **Physical and Water Damage:** 
- Physical damage (e.g., broken screens, bent devices) and water damage are **NOT** covered by the warranty.
    - If a device is received with pre-existing physical damage, the warranty is void.
- **Third-Party Repairs:** - Any unauthorized repairs or tampering by third parties without prior agreement from G-Fix Solution will **invalidate** the warranty.

## 2. Repair, Replace, and Refund Policy
- **Service Hierarchy:** 
- Our priority is to **Repair** first. If a repair is not possible, we will **Replace** the device. If a replacement is unavailable, a **Full Refund** will be issued.
- **Change of Mind:** G-Fix Solution reserves the right to **refuse a refund** if the customer simply changes their mind.
- **Restocking Fee:** All eligible non-defective returns are subject to a **15% restocking fee**.

## 3. NZ Consumer Guarantees Act (CGA)
- We comply with the **Consumer Guarantees Act 1993**. 
- Customers are entitled to a remedy (repair, replacement, or refund) if goods fail to meet guarantees.
- For "substantial" failures, you may reject the goods and choose a replacement or refund.
- Rights under the CGA are not limited by a fixed time but depend on the nature and price of the product.

## 4. Shipping and Returns Details
- **Delivery Damage:** If an order arrives damaged, notice must be sent within **3 days** for a free replacement.
- **Return Period:** Returns must be made within **7 days**.
- **Return Costs:** Products must be sent back at the **"Buyer's Expense"**. All courier and postage charges are non-refundable.
- **Condition:** Items must be in **re-sellable condition** to qualify for a refund.
- **Deposits & Layby:** Deposits are non-refundable. Cancellations of layby will result in deductions for loss in value.

## 5. Device Submission Requirements (Receiving Phones)
- **Proof of Purchase:** An original receipt may be required.
- **Lock Status:** We do **NOT** accept devices locked to a specific carrier or those with activation locks (e.g., iCloud/Google lock).
- **Accounts & Passwords:** - All existing accounts must be logged out. 
    - **All passwords and screen locks MUST be removed** before submission to allow our technicians access for testing.
